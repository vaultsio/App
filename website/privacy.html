Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 14, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to VaultSio. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we handle your personal data when you use our mobile application and tell you about your privacy rights.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, name, and authentication credentials
  • Financial Data: Transaction records, expense categories, wallet information, and budgets (stored securely in Firebase)
  • User Content: Notes, transaction descriptions, and receipt images you choose to upload

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features used, session duration, crash reports
  • Location Data: Only if you grant permission, for currency conversion features

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain our expense tracking service
  • Sync your data across devices using Firebase Cloud Storage
  • Send you budget alerts and notifications (if enabled)
  • Improve and optimize our app performance
  • Provide customer support
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase services, which are compliant with industry-standard security practices. All data transmission is encrypted using SSL/TLS protocols.

4.2 Security Measures

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive financial data
  • Optional app lock with PIN/biometric authentication
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Firebase Authentication for secure user management

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

5.1 We Do NOT Sell Your Data

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

5.2 We May Share Data With:

  • Service Providers: Firebase (Google Cloud) for data storage and authentication
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger or acquisition

6. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Export: Download your data in CSV or PDF format
  • Opt-out: Disable notifications and marketing communications

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to provide our services. If you delete your account, we will delete your data within 30 days, except where we are legally required to retain it.

8. Children's Privacy

VaultSio is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and stored on servers located outside your country. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this privacy policy.

10. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database, and cloud storage
  • Expo: App development framework
  • Currency Exchange APIs: For real-time currency conversion

Each service has its own privacy policy governing the use of your data.

11. Cookies and Tracking

The mobile app does not use cookies. However, our website may use cookies for analytics and to improve user experience. You can control cookie settings in your browser.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.

13. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@vaultsio.com
  • Website: https://vaultsio.com/contact
  • Address: San Francisco, CA

14. GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

If you are in the European Union, you have additional rights under GDPR:

  • Right to data portability
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

15. CCPA Compliance (California Users)

California residents have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected
  • Know whether personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Opt-out of the sale of personal information (Note: We do not sell personal information)
  • Request deletion of personal information
  • Non-discrimination for exercising CCPA rights

By using VaultSio, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.
